History

University of San Fransokyo is one of the world's leading schools. The school was built over 100 years and it is ranked #1 in the whole world. It is attended by various students who study in the fields of science and technology. Every year, the school holds a convention at its Exposition Hall, where up-and-coming geniuses are meant to showcase new creations to receive acceptance into the university.
